Mesa Security is a Game on the Source Engine where you play Andrew Henderson , a Former U.S. Army Major.

Mesa Security is inspired by Blue Shift even if we leave the Lλmbda complex.

The game takes place in the 2020s. Andrew saw the Black Mesa incident but from a completely different point of view. But, a little like Barney in Blue Shift. He's accompagned by Andrew Bell and Lily Liwen in this bizard adventure in the city of New Mesa sown with aliens and monsters of all kinds.
